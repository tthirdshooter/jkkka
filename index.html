Our History

The Jammu & Kashmir Kho Kho Association (JKKKA) has been the cornerstone of indigenous sports in the Union Territory for decades. Our journey began with local matches in Jammu and Srinagar and has evolved into a powerhouse that competes fiercely in the National Arena.

Affiliated with the Kho Kho Federation of India (KKFI) and J&K Sports Council, we work tirelessly to identify talent from rural districts and polish them for the international stage.
